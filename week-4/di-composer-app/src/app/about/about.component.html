Ludwig van Beethoven

Ludwig van Beethoven was a German composer that was born in 1770. He is one of the most famous composers in history and has many well known works. His musical talent was apparent from an early age. He studied with Mozart and took lessons with Joseph Hadyn. Famous for becomign deaf in his later years, he continued to make and perfrom music relying on vibrations.
